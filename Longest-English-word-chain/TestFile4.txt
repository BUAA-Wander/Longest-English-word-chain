Some animals make a huge difference on the stability of the ecosystem. Therefore,their quick extinct will be harmful to the environment by making food chain change and their **predator**'s number increase rapidly.For example,the decrease of the east-northern tiger make it much easier for many local small animals to breed and survive.Tremendous rabbits and sheep are flooding into everywhere in the east-northern district,destroying many plants and using a lot of water.Because of that,People live there are unable to get access to enough food and water while suffer from natural disaster more frequently.Similar examples around world are striking a lot of people every year like the wolf and sheep's story in Russia.
